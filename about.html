About muFX

Mission

muFX is an independent quantitative FX research operation. We deliver institutional-grade analysis of global currency markets, central bank policy, and the macro-structural forces that drive exchange rates.

We believe the best research comes from combining deep market knowledge with modern analytical tools. muFX uses AI-augmented intelligence systems to monitor sources, process data, and maintain the breadth of coverage typically associated with multi-analyst research desks — operated by a single analyst with skin in the game.

Coverage

G10 FX
EUR/USD, USD/JPY, GBP/USD, AUD/USD, NZD/USD, USD/CAD, USD/CHF — rates, positioning, and cross-currency dynamics.
EM FX
USD/CNH, Asian FX, LATAM currencies — managed regimes, carry trades, and political risk premia.
Central Banks
Fed, ECB, BOJ, BOE, PBOC, RBA — rate decisions, forward guidance, balance sheet policy, and leadership transitions.
Macro & Gold
Fiscal policy, trade policy, gold as a dollar confidence signal, reserve diversification, and positioning flows.

Publication Schedule

Quarterly outlooks at the start of each quarter. Flash notes on market-moving events. Deep dives on structural themes as they develop. All published at mufx.io.
